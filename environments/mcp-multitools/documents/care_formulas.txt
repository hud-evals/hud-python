======================================================================
PLANT CARE FORMULAS AND CALCULATIONS
======================================================================

DOCUMENT STRUCTURE
----------------------------------------
This document contains all formulas for calculating:
  1. Watering amounts based on conditions
  2. Nutrition amounts based on growth
  3. Expected growth rates
  4. Health score interpretations

Variables used in formulas:
  T = Average temperature (°C)
  H = Humidity (%)
  M = Soil moisture before watering (%)
  L = Light hours per day
  W = Current plant width (cm)
  Ht = Current plant height (cm)
  A = Days since last application
  P = Pot size (cm)

======================================================================

SECTION 1: WATERING FORMULAS
==================================================

Species: Monstera Deliciosa (Code: MON)
----------------------------------------
  Base Water Amount: 250 ml

  FORMULA W1 - Temperature Adjustment:
    Water_ml = 250 × (1 + (T - 22) × 0.03)
    Example: At 26°C: 250 × (1 + (26-22) × 0.03) = 280 ml

  FORMULA W2 - Moisture Adjustment:
    Water_ml = 250 × (1 + (40 - M) / 100)
    Example: At 30% moisture: 250 × (1 + (40-30)/100) = 275 ml

  FORMULA W3 - Combined (use this):
    Water_ml = 250 × (1 + (T - 22) × 0.03) × (1 + (40 - M) / 100) × (P / 20)

    Apply every 14-21 days during growing season

  FORMULA N2 - Growth-Based Adjustment:
    If weekly growth < expected: increase by 20%
    Expected weekly growth: 2.5 cm

  FORMULA N3 - Dilution Ratio:
    Standard dilution: 1:100 (fertilizer:water)

SECTION 3: GROWTH ESTIMATION
==================================================

Species: Monstera Deliciosa
----------------------------------------
  Base Growth Rate: 2.5 cm/week

  FORMULA G1 - Temperature Effect:
    Optimal range: 18-27°C
    Growth_factor = 1 - |T - 22| × 0.05

  FORMULA G2 - Light Effect:
    Optimal light: 6-10 hours/day
    If L < 6: Growth reduced by 30%
    If L > 10: Growth reduced by 10%

  FORMULA G3 - Weekly Height Estimate:
    New_height = Current_height + (2.5 × Growth_factor × Light_factor)

SECTION 4: HEALTH SCORE INTERPRETATION
==================================================

Score | Status         | Action Required
--------------------------------------------------
9-10  | Thriving       | Maintain current care
7-8   | Healthy        | No changes needed
5-6   | Needs Attention| Check water/light/nutrients
3-4   | Stressed       | Immediate intervention
1-2   | Critical       | Emergency care protocol

FORMULA H1 - Health Score Calculation:
  Base Score = 10
  Deductions:
    - Temperature outside optimal: -1 per 3°C deviation
    - Soil moisture <30% or >70%: -1
    - Growth rate <50% expected: -2
    - No fertilizer in 30+ days: -1
    - Pest/disease visible: -3


SECTION 5: QUICK REFERENCE COEFFICIENTS
==================================================

TEMPERATURE COEFFICIENTS (multiply water amount)
----------------------------------------
  < 15°C:  0.70
  15-18°C: 0.85
  18-22°C: 1.00
  22-26°C: 1.15
  26-30°C: 1.30
  > 30°C:  1.50

HUMIDITY COEFFICIENTS (multiply water amount)
----------------------------------------
  < 40%:   1.20
  40-50%:  1.10
  50-60%:  1.00
  60-70%:  0.90
  > 70%:   0.80

POT SIZE COEFFICIENTS (multiply all amounts)
----------------------------------------
  15 cm:   0.75
  20 cm:   1.00
  25 cm:   1.25
  30 cm:   1.50
