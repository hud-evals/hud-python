SPECIES: Monstera Deliciosa
Code: MON
==================================================

ENVIRONMENTAL REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------
  Temperature Range: 18-27°C
  Optimal Temperature: 22°C
  Light Requirements: 6-10 hours/day
  Soil pH Range: 5.5-7.0

WATER REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------
  Base Water Amount: 250 ml per watering
  Watering Frequency: Every 3 days (adjust based on conditions)
  Preferred Soil Moisture: 40-60%

NUTRITION REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------
  NPK Ratio: 10-10-10
  Fertilizing Frequency: Every 2-3 weeks

GROWTH CHARACTERISTICS
----------------------------------------
  Expected Growth Rate: 2.5 cm/week
  Monthly Growth Estimate: 10.0 cm/month

CARE NOTES
----------------------------------------
  - Likes to climb; provide moss pole for best growth
  - Fenestrations appear on mature leaves
  - Sensitive to cold drafts
