WESTBROOK LITERARY SOCIETY - STUDY CIRCLES
======================================================================

ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT
----------------------------------------
This document describes the active Study Circles within the Westbrook
Literary Society. Each circle focuses on a specific literary tradition
or theme. Members may belong to multiple circles. Circle Leaders are
responsible for setting reading schedules and facilitating discussions.

======================================================================

  - LS-1059 Julia Anderson
  - LS-1064 Oscar Fernandez

Current Study Text: The Art of War by Sun Tzu
Previous Texts: On War (Clausewitz), The Prince (Machiavelli)

  to modern life. Members are expected to prepare discussion points
  linking ancient wisdom to contemporary challenges.

CROSS-CIRCLE COLLABORATIONS

  Date: Last Sunday of each month, 4:00 PM
  Participating Members: All members from both circles

  Circles: The Romantics + The Regency Readers
  Topic: "Jane Austen's Revolutionary Heroines"
  Date: Second Wednesday of each month, 5:00 PM
  Participating Members: All members from both circles
